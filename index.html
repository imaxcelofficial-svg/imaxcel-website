About LAZOR Concept

LAZOR is my own car brand under IMAXCEL, designed and sketched by Joshua N.Hapption. Every curve, every line, every "Z" logo on the wheels is 100% my original artwork.


This concept car combines sleek aerodynamics, aggressive stance, and futuristic design. Built for Speed • Power • Future. Worth $1 Billion Vision.
