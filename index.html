About LAZOR

LAZOR is my original concept car design under IMAXCEL Future Automotive. Every curve, line, and detail you see here was sketched and designed by me, Joshua N. Hapption.

This concept represents Speed • Power • Future. The "Z" logo on the wheels, "LAZOR" branding on the body, and aggressive aerodynamic shape are all part of my vision for the next generation of electric hypercars.
