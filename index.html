About LAZOR

LAZOR is my original concept car under IMAXCEL Future Automotive. Every curve, every line, every "Z" logo you see was designed and sketched by me.

This concept represents the future: Speed • Power • Electric Performance. The LAZOR branding and aerodynamic design show my vision for next-gen hypercars.
